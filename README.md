# Cult of Cow - the Triangle

Welcome to the Cult of Cow - three cows are waiting to meet you:

  - Baby Cow can help you make friends! That you can eat!
  - Big Cow has a flower field to show you! Don't worry if he doesn't let you leave, that means he loves you!
  - Special Cow is special! He can make you feel happy! Just put him somewhere cold, and you can torture him!
